The Majestic Table

Objective

To design and develop a user-centered web app for 'The Majestic Table' that simplifies reservation and digital payments while delivering rich menu content and immersive visuals. The goal was to create an efficient, intuitive platform that enhances overall dining journey from discovery to payment.

Problem Statement & Project Goals

How might we design 'The Majestic Table' restaurant web app that allows users to effortlessly make reservations and payments, explore detailed menu information with good quality images, and easily communicate with the restaurant?

Comprehensive Menu

Allow users to access detailed menu descriptions.

High-Quality Visuals

Display vibrant images of the restaurant and dishes.

Easy Reservations

Develop a user-friendly reservation system.

Smooth Payments

Enhance payment process efficiency.

Quick Contact

Offer simple ways for users to communicate with the restaurant.

Customer Satisfaction

Improve overall satisfaction with modernized processes.

Research

A goal directed approach was taken into consideration to align the design of the web-app with user needs. Multiple users were interviewed about their experiences with other restaurant apps, identifying challenges, liked features and comfort with digital products, particularly for tasks like reservations, payments and menu browsing. Based on the data, hypothetical personas were created to guide the design process, ensuring a functional and user-centered final product.

Personas

Priyanka is a 34 year old marketing manager from Pune, India. She frequently dines at fancy restaurants. She is a busy professional who desires efficiency and comfort in her dining experiences. She relies of restaurant apps which would allow her to easily browse menus, make reservations and conduct secure payment transactions. She looks for a seamless reservation system and digital payment options, avoiding outdated processes and complicated cash transactions.

Radha Deol is a 26 year old food blogger from Bangalore, India. She has a large social media following. She aims to discover and review unique restaurants, shares detailed content and high-quality images with her audience. Radha seeks restaurant apps that provide detailed and clear menu descriptions, attractive visuals and easy contact options. She avoids app which tend have poor navigation or outdated information. She aims to grow her blog by delivering engaging, reliable content.

User Journey

Key Insights

The responsive web app for ‘The Majestic Table’ will allow customers to seamlessly make reservations, complete payments, access detailed menu descriptions, view high-quality visuals, and easily contact the restaurant which will enhance efficiency. It will allow to avoid frustrations with outdated processes and enable the creation of engaging content. Will measure effectiveness by tracking user satisfaction, interaction rates, and the volume of feedback provided through the app.

Value Proposition

The restaurant web-app for “The Majestic Table” provides these benefits and advantages after taking into consideration user's needs and frustrations.

Digital Wireframes

The digital wireframes were created to plan the basic structure of the web-app, allowing iterations and changes to be made easily before moving to high-fidelity designs.

Design Iterations

Improvements made after testing the low-fidelity prototype.

01
Confirmation Feedback

Users expected a confirmation message after completing actions.

Added visible success alerts to improve clarity and confidence.

02
Cart Update Issue

Cart quantity did not update clearly after removing items.

Implemented real-time cart count updates.

03
Payment Navigation

Users needed easier switching between payment methods.

Redesigned payment layout for smoother selection.

Accessibility

High Contrast

Text and backgrounds designed for easy readability.

Hierarchical Headings

Content structured so users can navigate logically.

Zoom-Friendly

Layouts dynamically resize for any screen or zoom level.

Clear Labels

Buttons and fields labeled clearly for all users.

Accessibility is at the heart of the design, ensuring every user enjoys a seamless experience.

Takeaway

The web-app designed and developed for the fancy restaurant “The Majestic Table” provided valuable insights into crafting a solution which would meet the needs of users. The goal was to create a web-app which would provide easy and efficient payment processes as well as detailed menu descriptions. Additionally, create an intuitive and seamless app experience for the users. It was a great experience in designing such a web-app. I personally learnt to empathize with the users during the process. The insights and feedback provided by the users based on the low-fidelity prototypes really helped me build a user-centered solution. While developing the web-app I understood the importance of accessibility and how it can be incorporated throughout the designs due to which the app would be accessible to huge range of users. I am eager to design and develop more solutions while expanding and applying my knowledge.
